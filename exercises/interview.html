Given this function of a parabola:

y = x^2 + -2 * Hx + H * H + K

What is the parabola's vertex?

( H , K )

a point, like (-5, 3)

y = x^2 + -2 * Hx + H * H + K

y - H * H + K = x^2 + -2 * Hx

y - H * H + K + H * H = x^2 + -2 * Hx + H * H

y - K = x^2 + -2 * Hx + H * H

y - K = (x - H)^2

y - \blue{(K)} = (x - \green{(H)})^2

The vertex is (\green{H}, \blue{K}).
